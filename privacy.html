Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tread The Needle collects, uses, and protects information when you use the mobile game. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

The app may collect or process the following types of information:

  • Device identifiers, such as IDFV and advertising identifiers when permitted by the user.
  • Gameplay and product interaction data, such as level progress, ad availability, and reward events.
  • Advertising data used to request, display, measure, and attribute ads.
  • Diagnostics data, such as crash information and performance data.

How We Use Information

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • Providing app functionality, saving progress, and improving gameplay stability.
  • Serving rewarded advertisements and measuring ad performance.
  • Fraud prevention, security, abuse detection, and service protection.
  • Analytics and diagnostics to understand technical issues and improve the app.

Advertising and Tracking

The app uses third-party advertising SDKs to provide rewarded ads. On iOS, the app requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before accessing data that may be used for tracking. If you decline tracking permission, the app will continue to function, though advertising personalization and attribution may be limited.

Third-Party Services

The app may integrate third-party services, including advertising and security SDKs. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies and platform requirements. The app only initializes advertising services when ad configuration is available.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the app, meet operational needs, prevent fraud, resolve issues, and comply with legal obligations.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in a way that is not permitted by applicable law. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Your Choices

You may manage tracking permission in iOS Settings. You may also reset advertising identifiers or delete the app to remove locally stored game data from your device.

Contact
