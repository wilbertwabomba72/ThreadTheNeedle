About the Game

Tread The Needle is a minimalist reflex game about timing, precision, and patience. Each level asks you to launch a ball through rotating rings and line up the openings at the right moment. Later stages introduce multiple rings, different sizes, offset centers, changing speeds, and opposite rotation directions.

Features

  • Single, double, and triple ring challenges.
  • Increasingly varied layouts and movement patterns.
  • Pause and countdown resume controls.
  • Rewarded revive flow for optional second chances.
  • Minimal visual style designed for quick, readable play.
